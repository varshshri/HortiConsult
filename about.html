About Dr. Aswath

Dr. Chenna Reddy Aswath is a visionary leader in Indian horticulture, having served as Principal Scientist at ICAR-IIHR for over three decades. His groundbreaking research in sustainable agriculture, urban gardening, and organic farming has revolutionized how we approach horticulture in India.

As the CEO of Krishika Consultancy, Dr. Aswath leads a team of expert horticulturists, scientists, and specialists dedicated to bringing cutting-edge agricultural solutions to farmers, urban gardeners, and agribusinesses. His expertise spans from traditional farming methods to modern technologies like hydroponics and vertical farming.

Beyond his scientific contributions, Dr. Aswath is a passionate educator and community leader. He serves as President of the Lions Club, Bangalore Agara chapter, and has established educational programs that have trained thousands of students in sustainable agriculture practices.
